### Motivation
* 解决软件碎片化问题
* 支持异构硬件编译
* 降低构建领域特定编译器的成本
* 连接现有编译器


### Design principles
***Little Builtin, Everything Customizable \[Parsimony\]***

***SSA and Regions \[Parsimony\]***

***Maintain Higher-Level Semantics \[Progressivity\]***

***Declaration and Validation \[Parsimony\]***

***Source Location Tracking \[Traceability\]***
